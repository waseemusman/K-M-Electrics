Welcome to K & M Electrics 1, Inc.
 

K & M Electrics 1, Inc is located in Orlando, FL and incorporated in 2002. We are a family-orientated company that is committed in delivering the highest quality service performance, professionalism and dependability to all of our customers. Fortified with an electrical skill-set handed down from father to son for five generations, coupled with the book smarts of military education and modern practices. K & M Electrics 1, Inc expertise are in commercial and Industrial structures, control circuits, custom designs circuits, generators, service upgrades, educational, medical, religious, entertainment and transit facilities. We also specializes in LED retrofit services, and facilities maintenance programs.Our experience in controls has led us into power plants controls, industrial automation, and manufacturing plants.

K & M Electrics 1 Inc, conducts our business with the highest level of integrity, safety, performance and productivity. Consistently focus on the highest quality production by committing to excellence at the lowest possible cost.
